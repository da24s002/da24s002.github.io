I am an M.S. Research Scholar in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras), Chennai, India. 

My research focuses on investigating the theoretical and empirical equivalence between Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) and boosting algorithms. I am also developing advanced Boosting architectures that utilize neural routers for intelligent spatial partitioning to handle heteroscedastic noise.

Prior to joining IIT Madras, I worked as a Specialist Programmer at Infosys, where I architected serverless data pipelines and optimized enterprise REST APIs. I earned my B.Tech in Computer Science from RCC Institute of Information Technology, graduating with a CGPA of 9.33/10.

My technical expertise spans across C++, Python, PyTorch, AWS, and MLOps tools like MLflow, DVC, Prometheus, and Grafana.
